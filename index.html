About me

Kamil is the CEO and Co-founder of Dropifi, a fast-growing leads generation and customer engagement software startup.

Though Kamil’s background in Law and Economics relate to the built environment having originally studied (BSc.) Land Economy at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (Ghana), he is fascinated with web-based technology in solving small business problems. This passion led him to further study Software Development and Entrepreneurship at the Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of technology.

He co-founded Dropifi to help online businesses grow whiles gaining valuable customer insights. The company was the global winner of Kauffman Foundation’s Startup Open Competition in 2012 and later became the first African-founded company to be accepted into the 500 Startups accelerator in Silicon Valley.

Kamil directs the vision and overall corporate strategy of the company as well as directly involved with Marketing, Business Development and Growth.
